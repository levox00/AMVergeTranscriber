# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in the AMVerge community a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone.

We are committed to creating an open, welcoming, and constructive environment regardless of experience level, identity, background, or skill level.

## Expected Behavior

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and professional
- Accepting constructive feedback
- Helping others learn
- Communicating clearly and patiently
- Focusing on what is best for the project

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or discrimination
- Insults, personal attacks, or trolling
- Spam or disruptive behavior
- Publishing private information without permission
- Repeated hostile or bad-faith interactions

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards and may remove, edit, or reject contributions, comments, issues, or other interactions that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including:

- GitHub issues
- Pull requests
- Discussions
- Community spaces related to the project

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the project maintainer.

## Consequences

Maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans from the community.
